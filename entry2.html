Target Audience

Three guiding principles

1. Gift Inspiration: This principle provides users to choose the perfect flower for any event or person by offering personalized suggestions for meaningful floral gifts based on the symbolism.

2. Symbolism Database: This principle offers users to learn and add new valuable information about plants by providing resources of plants.

3. Plant Selection Guide: This principle allows users to search plants that match with their emotions or occasions which helps to create a more engaging and interacting experience.

These principles explore meaningful, inspirational, and educational sites which stand out from typical floral websites.
