Using Floral Type in the Mantra Merch Project

I included my Mantra Merch project on this website to highlight my deep connection to flowers and their influence on my design choices. The font Dalliance IT Flourishes, used in this project, evokes an art nouveau and nature-inspired aesthetic that perfectly aligns with the essence of blooms and organic beauty.

A mantra is “a sound, syllable, word, or group of words considered capable of creating transformation.” For this project, I selected a personal mantra—something to inspire or reflect upon daily—and designed wearable merchandise to embody it. I chose the typefaces The Seasons and Dalliance IT Flourishes from Adobe Fonts for their ability to convey the transformative and seasonal meaning behind the mantra. This combination creates a seamless connection between the natural world and the power of words.
